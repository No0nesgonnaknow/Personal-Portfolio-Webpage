Peking Duck Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 whole duck, about 5-6 pounds
  • 2 tablespoons Chinese five-spice powder
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6-8 green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 16-20 Mandarin pancakes
  • Hoisin sauce, for serving
  • Cucumber slices, for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C).
  2. Rinse the duck inside and out, then pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together Chinese five-spice powder, honey, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and salt to create a marinade.
  4. Brush the marinade all over the duck, inside and out. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
  5. Place the duck on a wire rack set over a baking sheet. Stuff the cavity with green onion pieces.
  6. Bake the duck in the preheated oven for 2 to 2 1/2 hours, or until the skin is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the duck from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before carving.
  8. To serve, slice the duck meat and crispy skin. Serve with Mandarin pancakes, hoisin sauce, and cucumber slices.
