Moussaka Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Slice the eggplants into 1/4-inch thick rounds. Brush them with olive oil and arrange them on a baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes until tender. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion is soft and translucent.
  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk constantly for 1-2 minutes to make a roux.
  7. Gradually add the milk to the roux, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Spread half of the eggplant slices in the bottom of a baking dish. Top with half of the meat mixture and sprinkle with half of the grated Parmesan cheese.
  9. Add another layer of the remaining eggplant slices, followed by the remaining meat mixture and grated Parmesan cheese.
  10. Pour the white sauce over the top and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  12. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.
