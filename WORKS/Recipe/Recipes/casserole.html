Zucchini Pizza Casserole Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a casserole dish.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent.
  4. Add sliced zucchini to the skillet and cook until slightly tender.
  5. Season with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  6. Spread half of the zucchini mixture in the greased casserole dish.
  7. Pour half of the marinara sauce over the zucchini layer.
  8. Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  9. Arrange half of the sliced pepperoni on top.
  10. Repeat the layers with the remaining zucchini, marinara sauce, cheeses, and pepperoni.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
