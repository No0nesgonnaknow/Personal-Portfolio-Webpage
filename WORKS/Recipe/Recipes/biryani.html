Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup ghee or vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash the Basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat ghee or vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for another minute.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  6. Now, add cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add plain yogurt and mix everything together. Cover the pan and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  8. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and add soaked rice. Cook until the rice is 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  9. In a large ovenproof dish, layer the partially cooked rice and the chicken mixture, starting and ending with a layer of rice.
  10. Cover the dish tightly with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves before serving.
