Tacos Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 12 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • Lime wedges for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Add the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, cayenne pepper (if desired), and salt. Stir well to combine all the spices with the meat.
  4. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend together.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave.
  6. Place a spoonful of the meat mixture onto each tortilla.
  7. Add shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, chopped cilantro, and sour cream on top of the meat.
  8. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the tacos for added flavor.
  9. Fold the tortillas in half or roll them up to form tacos.
  10. Serve immediately and enjoy!
