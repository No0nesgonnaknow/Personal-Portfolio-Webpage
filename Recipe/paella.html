Paella Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 pound chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large paella pan or skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Saut√© until onions become translucent.
  3. Add sliced red and green bell peppers. Cook for a few minutes until peppers soften.
  4. Add Arborio rice and stir to coat the rice with oil.
  5. Pour in white wine and cook until it evaporates.
  6. Add diced tomatoes, smoked paprika, and saffron threads. Stir well.
  7. Gradually add chicken or vegetable broth while stirring continuously.
  8. Add chicken thighs and season with salt and pepper. Stir gently.
  9. Cover the pan and let it simmer for about 15 minutes or until the rice is almost cooked.
  10. Add shrimp and frozen peas. Stir gently to combine.
  11. Cover the pan again and cook for an additional 5 minutes or until the shrimp is cooked and the rice is tender.
  12. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  13. Garnish with lemon wedges before serving.
