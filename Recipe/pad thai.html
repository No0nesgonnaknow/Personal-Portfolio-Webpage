Pad Thai Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 8 ounces shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup tofu, diced
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for about 10 minutes or until they become pliable. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Push the garlic to one side of the skillet and add the beaten eggs to the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked.
  5. Add shrimp, bean sprouts, tofu, and sliced green onions to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the shrimp is cooked through.
  6. Add the soaked rice noodles to the skillet and toss everything together.
  7. In a small bowl, mix together fish sauce, tamarind paste, sugar, and chili powder (if using). Pour the sauce over the noodles and toss to coat evenly.
  8. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the noodles are heated through.
  9. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped roasted peanuts and fresh cilantro.
  10. Serve hot with lime wedges on the side.
