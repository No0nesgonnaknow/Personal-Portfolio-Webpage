Sushi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 4 nori seaweed sheets
  • 200g fresh sushi-grade fish (such as salmon, tuna, or yellowtail), thinly sliced
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Soy sauce for dipping
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger for serving

Steps

  1. Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  2. Spread the cooked rice onto a large flat dish or a sushi mat. Leave about 1 inch of space at the top.
  3. Place a sheet of nori seaweed on top of the rice. Wet your hands with water to prevent sticking and press the rice firmly onto the nori, leaving a small border at the bottom.
  4. Arrange the sliced fish, cucumber, and avocado in a line at the bottom edge of the nori.
  5. Starting from the bottom, tightly roll the sushi using your hands or a sushi mat. Apply gentle pressure to ensure a compact roll.
  6. Moisten the top border of the nori with water to seal the roll.
  7. Repeat the process for the remaining ingredients.
  8. Using a sharp knife, slice each sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
  9. Serve the sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
